GAME MECHANICS

Mother Egg

Mother Egg gives you 0.0000005 MotherEGG Size per BNB, which translates to a Size of 2 000 for 1 finney, and a Size of 2 000 000 for 1 BNB.

The minimum requirement to Mother Egg is 1 finney (0.001 BNB).


Mother Eggt gives you 1 Egg per MotherEGG Size, multiplied by the global bonus.

The global bonus increases by +1% for every second since anyone Mother Egg.

For example, buying mother egg with 1 BNB after 1 hour (3600 seconds) would give you: 2MM * (100% + 1% * 3600) = 74MM Eggs.


Mother Egg forfeits any unclaimed Harvest!

Harvest Share

Harvest Share transfers unclaimed BNB to the player's ingame balance.

BNB rewards accumulate at a rate of 4% of the MotherEGG Size value per day.


Harvest Share gives 1 Egg per MotherEGG Size per day, multiplied by personal bonus and Boost.

The personal bonus starts at 4, and increases by 1 per hour since the player's last MotherEGG action (Invoke, Upgrade or Harvest).

The Boost is a fixed multiplier obtained through Upgrade MotherEGG.

For example, Harvesting with a MotherEGG Size of 2MM and a Boost of 1 after 1 hour (3600 seconds) would give you: 2MM * 1 * (4 + 1) / 86400 = 416 666 Eggs

Upgrade MotherEGG

Upgrade MotherEGG uses the BNB share to Upgrade MotherEGG Size at a rate identical to mother egg.

The player also receives the same amount of Eggs as for Harvest Share.


If it has been at least one hour since the player's last MotherEGG action, the player gets a Boost.

For each extra hour, the player also gets a Boost, up to a maximum of +10 at once.

For example, Upgrade the MotherEGG after 5 hours 30 minutes will give the player +5 Boost.

Sell Egg

Sell Egg trades as many Eggs as the player wants and sends BNB in return.

BNB received is equal to: (Wonkers' Pot) * (Egg Gift) / (Eggs To Win) / 2.

No more than 50% of the wonkPot can be won in one gift.


Eggs from the gift are added to the global Eggs sells variable.

If Eggs sells surpass Eggs To Win, the player wins the round.

He receives this round's jackpot (20% of the reserve jackpot), and the next round begins.

New Round

Eggs Sells reset to 0, and Eggs To Win are set according to the jackpot.

Eggs to Win = 1 Billion for 0.1 BNB in the Jackpot (or 10MM for 1 finney).

Both the jackpot and Eggs to Win change dynamically through the round as players buy mother eggs.


The first time a player from a previous round takes a MotherEGG action, their Boost is reset to 0.

Their MotherEGG Size is also lowered by 20% for every round they've skipped.

For example, if we're at Round 3 and a Round 1 player didn't play Round 2, their MotherEGG Size of 4000 will become 4000 * 0.8 * 0.8 = 2560


Unclaimed share remains available, but in proportion with the new MotherEGG Size.

It's best to Harvest before the round ends!

Pot Split

On every payable transaction, the incoming BNB is split as follows:


- 40% to the MotherEGGPot, holds payouts from MotherEGG Size

- 30% to the wonkPot, reserve for Egg Trades

- 20% to the jackPot, 20% of that rewarded to the Round winner

- 10% to the CryptoEGGPot, to be sent to CryptoEGG Contract at your convenience
